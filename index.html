ABOUT

I have the master’s degree in Character Animation in Bristol University in UK. I worked over 10 years as a lecture in Zhongyuan University of Technology in China. I taught the courses including 2D& 3D Computer Design, Computer Graphic, Post-production, Video Editing, 3D Animation, 3D Modelling and so on. I am familiar with many software for design including Maya, 3ds Max, Animate, Premiere, After Effects, Illustrator, Audition, InVision, XD, Bridge, InDesign, Photoshop and so on. Furthermore, I have some works and received several awards for these works.

My specialty is 3D Animation, 2D Animation, Motion Graphic Animation and Graphic Design. I have a lot of experience in such fields. In the study of the program of Interactive Media Management in Algonquin College, I’ve improved the ability of Web Design, Web Development and UX Design. I learned some programming language including Html, CSS, Java and PHP. I can design and build a whole web by myself. Through the intensive study, I became a highly dependable, efficient and detail-oriented person. I learned to manage my time well and worked efficiently as a part of a team.
